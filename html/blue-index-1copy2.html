Shoplium.

We understand that a considerable amount of time and money is lost in the process of converting altcoins to fiat currency, just to make a purchase on the World Wide Web.

For this reason, Shoplium is here to provide the first multi-currency platform to allow users to purchase anywhere online, and do so with over 20 major crypto-currencies.

The Platform.

In essence, the Shoplium platform allows its users to deposit and exchange over 20 crypto-currencies, as well as place orders online, track deliveries, and more.

  • Purchase Anywhere on the Web.
  • BTC and Over 20 Altcoins Accepted as Payment.
  • Products Delivered Directly to the Door.
  • Order Tracking.
  • Speedy Customer Support.
  • Loyalty Programs.
